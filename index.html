Your story matters.
We are a digital museum of real individuals' mental health stories.
India has the world’s largest population of young people ages 10-24 and mental health problems are the leading health concern for this group.
We all have mental health, just like physical health. It keeps changing and needs to be looked after and maintained.
Looking after our mental health can not only enable us to be resilient and lead fulfilling lives, but also helps us cope better with the inevitable difficulties and problems, both big and small.
