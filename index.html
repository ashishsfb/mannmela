Mann Mela is a public engagement project that aims to raise youth awareness about mental health through immersive multimedia and storytelling.
We wish to build an interactive exhibit to evoke empathy, self- reflection and help the viewers understand Mental health.
We have coducted co-creation workshops, interviews over the past one year to dig deep into the space of mental health and find real stories
All the the exhibits and experiences at Mann Mela are based on personal narratives and stories that young people have shared with us
