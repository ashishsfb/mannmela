Mann Mela is a public engagement project by It’s Ok To Talk, Sangath, that aims to raise youth awareness about mental health through immersive multimedia and storytelling.
We want young people to know that mental health is an extremely positive and personal asset and that mental health spans across all aspects of our lives.
This project is being collaboratively designed by a team of young people, including those with lived experiences, researchers and experts, designers, artists and technologists.
Mann Mela will share interactive exhibits telling the stories of young people from India. Mann Mela museum events will be conducted from July - December 2020.
